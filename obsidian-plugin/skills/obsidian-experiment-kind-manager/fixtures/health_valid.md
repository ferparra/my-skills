## Question

What effect does 300mg Magnesium Glycinate have on sleep latency and morning HRV?

## Hypothesis

I believe Magnesium Glycinate will reduce sleep latency by 10+ minutes and improve
HRV by 5+ points due to its role in GABA modulation and nervous system downregulation.

## Method

Take 300mg Magnesium Glycinate 30 minutes before bed for 30 days. Track sleep
latency and HRV nightly via Oura Ring. Maintain consistent 22:30 sleep time.

## Metrics

- Sleep latency (minutes) — from lights out to sleep onset (Oura)
- Morning HRV (ms) — first reading after waking
- Subjective morning energy (1–10) — logged in daily note within 5 minutes of waking

## Interventions

- 300mg Magnesium Glycinate 30 minutes before target sleep time

## Controls

- Consistent sleep onset target: 22:30
- No alcohol during 30-day window
- Consistent wake time: 06:30

## Confounders

- Work stress levels (tracked with daily stress score 1–10)
- Travel or time zone changes (will pause experiment if travel >2h timezone shift)
- Exercise volume variation (logged in training note)
